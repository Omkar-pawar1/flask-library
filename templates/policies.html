Library Policies

Borrowing Policy

Our library allows registered users to borrow books according to the following policies:

  • Each user can borrow a maximum of 5 books at a time.
  • The borrowing period for each book is 15 days.
  • Renewals can be requested up to two times, provided there are no holds on the book.

Librarian Policies

Librarians are responsible for managing the library's operations and enforcing the following policies:

  • Librarians must maintain accurate records of borrowed and returned books.
  • Librarian can add,delete,update sections
  • Librarian can add,delete,update book under section

Return Policy

Users are required to adhere to the following guidelines when returning borrowed books:

  • Books must be returned on or before the due date to avoid overdue fines.
  • If user dosen't return the book system will automatically block the access of books which are overdue
